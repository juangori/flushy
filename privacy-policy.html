Flushy Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Overview

Flushy ("the App") is a personal gut health tracking application. Your privacy is fundamental to how Flushy is designed. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

Data Collection

Flushy does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All health-related data you enter into Flushy — including stool type, color, tags, and timestamps — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. This data never leaves your phone.

What We Do NOT Do

  • We do not collect personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • We do not require account creation or login
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs
  • We do not transmit any data over the internet
  • We do not use cookies or similar technologies
  • We do not share data with third parties
  • We do not access your device's health data (Apple Health, Google Fit)

Local Data Storage

Your tracking data is saved locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data persists between app sessions but is removed if you uninstall the App. You can delete your data at any time from within the App.

Data Export

Flushy may offer the ability to export your data. When you export, the data is shared using your device's native sharing capabilities. Flushy does not retain or transmit copies of exported data.

Children's Privacy

Flushy does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The App does not collect any personal information from any user regardless of age.

Security

Since all data remains on your device and is never transmitted externally, your information is protected by your device's own security measures (passcode, biometrics, encryption).

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
